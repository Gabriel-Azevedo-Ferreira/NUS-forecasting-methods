#Predicting Foreign Exchange Rates

We try to predict Japanese yen currency based on past data about the following currencies:

1. AUD/USD: Australian dollor vs US dollor
2. GBP/USD: British Pound vs US dollor
3. CAD/USD: Canadian dollor vs US dollor
4. NLG/USD: Dutch Guilders vs US dollor
5. FRF/USD: French franc vs US dollor
6. DEM/USD: German marks vs US dollor
7. JPY/USD: Japanese yen vs US dollor
8. CHF/USD: Swiss franc vs US dollor
